About Us

Our History

Campanella Pizza began its journey in 2014 as a small family dream to bring authentic Italian-style pizza to the heart of Meyerton. What started as a passion for traditional wood-fired cooking has grown into a beloved local establishment that serves the community with pride.

Our founders recognised that Meyerton needed a place where families could gather over genuinely delicious, freshly made pizza. They invested in a traditional wood-fired oven and committed to using only the finest locally sourced ingredients to create something special.

Over the years, we have become more than just a pizzeria. We are a cornerstone of the community, where neighbors become friends and every meal is prepared with love and attention to detail.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic, high-quality, and freshly made pizza using locally sourced ingredients, while providing exceptional service in a warm and welcoming environment.

Our Vision

To become Meyerton's most loved local pizzeria by maintaining excellence in food and service and expanding into nearby communities.

Meet the Team

Our Owners

Campanella Pizza is proudly owned by two dedicated individuals who share a passion for authentic Italian cuisine and exceptional customer service. Their vision and commitment to quality have made Campanella Pizza a beloved part of the Meyerton community since 2014.

Our Chefs

Our two experienced chefs bring years of culinary expertise to every pizza we create. Trained in traditional wood-fired cooking techniques, they ensure that each pizza is crafted to perfection, maintaining the authentic flavors and quality that our customers have come to love.

Our General Staff

Our team of five dedicated general staff members work tirelessly to provide you with exceptional service. From taking your orders to ensuring your dining experience is memorable, our friendly staff are committed to making every visit to Campanella Pizza a pleasant one.
